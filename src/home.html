The Redeeming Politics Challenge

1. Become more educated on political issues

Visit our Political Education tab and take a few quizzes to see where your political views lie. Whenever you don't know much about an issue, look it up! We recommend using a variety of sources to find your information, discussed further in the Understanding Bias section. Then use our search feature to find polls near you to exercise your right to vote.

2. Become more aware of polarization

Visit our Understanding Bias tab and learn more about how bias impacts all of our lives. If we don't know or acknowledge it's there, we become more accustomed to it and become more polarized ourselves.

3. Normalize discourse with the other side

Visit the Normalizing Dialogue section on our website to learn more about tips on starting the dialogue with your peers who don't agree with you, finding local political events near you, and even signing up to chat with someone of an alternate political background.
